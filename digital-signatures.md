# Digital Signatures and Authentication

## Digital Signatures

Digital signatures help guarantee:

- Authenticity
- Integrity
- Non-repudiation

They confirm that data was not modified during transmission.

---

## RSA

RSA is widely used for:

- Encryption
- Digital signatures
- Secure communication

---

## WebAuthn

WebAuthn is a modern authentication standard based on cryptographic keys.

Benefits:
- Reduced password dependence
- Stronger authentication
- Phishing resistance

---

## Authentication Security

Modern authentication mechanisms reduce risks related to:

- Credential theft
- Password reuse
- Phishing attacks

---

## Key Takeaway

Strong authentication mechanisms are critical for modern cybersecurity.
