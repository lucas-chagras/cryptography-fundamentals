# Encryption in Transit vs Encryption at Rest

## Encryption in Transit

Encryption in transit protects data while it moves between systems.

Examples:
- HTTPS
- VPNs
- TLS

---

## End-to-End Encryption

End-to-end encryption ensures only the communicating parties can access the information.

Even service providers cannot read the protected content.

---

## Encryption at Rest

Encryption at rest protects stored data.

Examples:
- Full-Disk Encryption
- Encrypted databases
- Protected backups

---

## Secure Deletion

Secure deletion helps prevent recovery of sensitive data after removal.

---

## Quantum Computing

Quantum computing introduces new challenges to modern cryptography.

Quantum bits (qubits) may represent multiple states simultaneously, potentially impacting current encryption algorithms in the future.

---

## Key Takeaway

Encryption is essential for protecting sensitive information both during transmission and storage.
